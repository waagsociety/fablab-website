This project is my bachelor thesis of the course Product Design @ HvA.

My assignment is to design a contemporary chandelier made from handcrafted Venitian / Murano glass. The arms of the traditional chandelier form as a starting point in this design process; they are formed using a specific technique (placement in a horizontal mold). By reinventing this technique and using modernized elements in the design a contemporary chandelier will be created.
