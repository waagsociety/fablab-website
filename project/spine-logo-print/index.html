Spine logo print
mjiles04
Tue, 11/10/2015

I had an idea to create a Spine logo and print it on the back of a T-shirt. The vinyl cutter can use Illustrator vector images. I had no idea how to do it, but the team showed me. Once I got the hang of it, I cut several examples, in different sizes.Â 

To print the image on the T-shirt, I used a black fabric ink, the spine stencil from the vinyl cutter, and a sponge. I put the cutout on the back of the shirt and dabbed the sponge over the open space. The result is shown in the photograph.
