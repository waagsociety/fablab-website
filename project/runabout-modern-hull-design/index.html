For a private project I am designing a runabout (fast planing boat) with a classic look above the waterline and a modern underwater shape. After developing the concept (please see files attached) in Blender and other dedicated software (DelftShip) it is time to move the proect to the tangible phase:

I want to cut the principle hull shape to get a better understanding of its dimensions and the "ergonomics" of the design and planned components. So this project is about converting dreams to physical reality!

As I am in dept to many freeware programs and shared contents on the web, I am happy to share files with interested parties. Please contact me if you are interested.
