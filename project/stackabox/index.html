Tue, 02/10/2015

I will attempt to create a box where you can put stuff in.

Many of the designs I've found (e.g. on Thingiverse) only have some of my desiderata. Here I'll try to satisfy them all:
- Laser-cutter design files (so I can make bigger-sized versions in less time).
- Parametric (so I can change dimensions according to different space needs/contraints, material thickness or fabrication tolerances).
- Stackable (if two boxes are one on top of the other, the bottom one should prevent the top one to move sideways).
- Fun for me to design (I'm a developer and I like when things come out from an algorithm :) ).
