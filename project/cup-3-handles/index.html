Cup with 3 handles
Jurre Luuk en Joris
Mon, 01/09/2012

As an experiment, we have chosen to print a cup with three handles with the Ultimaker 3D printer. As we had to create one design with three people, we thought of this idea, to personalize the three handles. They all had their own 'character'. However, as the photos do show, this did not complete materialize in the way we had planned. This was partly caused by the difficulty of the design we used.
