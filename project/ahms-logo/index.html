Embroidering text: note that the font is way bigger then you prefer the original to be. Maybe even two sizes bigger, and inside enough white space also for the rest of the lines that are close.

Fine embroidery
Setting up the embroidery machine takes a while since the design needs to go from JPG into the software of Janome. This software (in the end) puts several folders on your USB stick. And make sure you have also the different filename extensions there: .eof .edr .jef and .bak.

Only then remove the stick from the computer and put it in the embroidery machine while it is not on yet. And always turn off embroidery machine before removing USB stick.

Enjoy!
