Bar stool
dr_jerry
Thu, 10/29/2015

My colleague needs to be agile and that means a daily stand up, too much for his old bones. So I decide to create a standup stool, also very usable as barstool.Â 

It's a derivative from x-stool and made from wasted plywood collected from the bins of one the Noord-Zuidlijn's building spots. And can be customized and downloaded from stretchsketch.com

Because the material is used concrete plywood (betonplex) it needed to be cleansed thoroughly. I brought my own 6mm milling bit, so Fablab's bits wouldn't be damaged in case some concrete parts are left on the sheet.

The milling time was approx 1,5 hour but I think can be reduced by increasing the cut depth.

Thanks to Roger for assisting me and a perfect time at Waag!
