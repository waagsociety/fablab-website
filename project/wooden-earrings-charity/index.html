Wooden earrings for charity

Thu, 05/15/2014

I wanted to do charity work for as long as I can remember. I have always been very creative and wanted to do something with that to help. I have been to Indonesia a few times and saw how hard is was for people there. Last year I decided that I wanted to go to Indonesia to work in different orphanages. I have been collecting money and clothes to help abandoned children.

This is one of the projects I'm doing. I'm going to different markets and sell handmade jewelry, clothes and food. All the income goes to charity.

I made a lot of different earring designs. They are all 1x1 cm of 1,2x1,2 cm big. This was the first time I ever worked with wood(3 mm). I used the lasercutter to cut and engrave the wood.
