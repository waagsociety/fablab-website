3D Car with low drag
daanl
Thu, 10/03/2013

For a school assignment we needed to do a project. The subject was free to chose, so I decided to do it about a car with an as low as possible drag.

I first made a 3D model of a car with a low dragcoefficient which is currently on the road (Nissan GTR). Then I designed my own car with a potentially even lower dragcoefficient. The car however, needed to be usable for daily use so space for at least 4 persons, room for luggage and needed to have 4 wheels.
