Panel House Study
danimaravi
Wed, 01/07/2015

The study consists in making a model house to test different possibilities in the dwelling's principal space. Located in the Caribe, the intention is to create a in-out space using a paneled facade. We will lasercut different panel options to test wich one suits better in the house.
