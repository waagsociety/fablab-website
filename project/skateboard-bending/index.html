Bending of a skateboard at Fablab Amsterdam

Process with wood bending machine:
- fill the tube with water
- attach the pipe to the wooden box
- plug in the electricity to heat it up
- start bending 

This is how it went: 

[14:00] I got help from Cecilia, who explained how it works and what to do
[14:15] Started the wood bending machine. Now I just have to wait.
[14:23] 20 degrees (I am waiting till it is 80 degrees)
[14:41] 50 degrees
[14:55] 80 degrees
[14:56] Putting in the skateboards
[14:58] Because I opened the doors, temperature declined to 70 degrees
[15:01] 85 degrees
[15:07] 90 degrees
[15:12] I tried to bend one of the two skateboards.
[15:18] One skateboard is tightened up
[15:25] 95 degrees again
[15:29] The second skateboard also looks good, so I’ve tightened that one too.
[17:00] Result of the first skateboard is good! :-)
[17:14] Result of the second skateboard is also good

So, it takes about three hours, but you will end up with a nicely bended skateboard.
