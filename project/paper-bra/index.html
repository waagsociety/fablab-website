Paper Bra
bodiljane
Wed, 10/12/2011

by Bodil Jane Kleipool

I've designed this bra for a fashion project about flowers and plants. My greatest inspiration came from the paper flower hats shown in the Summer Spring Collection 2009 of Chanel. I've tried to make the paper look like lace as much as I could. Cutting out the patterns by hand took me quite a long time so i'm very happy that I could use the lasercutter at the Fablab.

The machine could work so much more efficient and refined, producing even better results than I hoped for. I've been able to experiment with different materials such as felt, fake leather and different kinds of paper. I love the way the felt version didn't came out perfect because of the fine detailed patterns. The laser burned out some of the felt, creating an even more vulnerable result.

To see what's happening next with the paper bra, check out my Website.
