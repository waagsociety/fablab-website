Extracting fragrance out of raw materials

This year I have been part of the reseachlab group "how devices define a landscape", in the bulb flower region "Hogeveense Polder".

This region is famous and very much appreciated for its colorful fields during the flower season. This scenery however is very temporary and actually a side effect of the production of bulbs, that are primarily meant for export. In my research I looked for the remains of this production and tried to grasp what was left on the field.

Through extraction, I was able to collect some interesting fragrances from this field. With the new season coming, I will be setting up a mobile lab and process the raw material directly on the field. In the open wetlab, I wish to gain more knowledge and experience on distillation.
