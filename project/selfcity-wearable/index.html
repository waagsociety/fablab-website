SelfCity Wearable

Jens
Sat, 02/23/2008

SelfCity: SelfCity is a serious game for adolescents in the age of 13-15 with a social skills disability to practice social skills. The pupils are often emotionally impaired as the result of ADHD, PDD, NOS or neglect. In the game the pupils are accompanied by a buddy (deamon). This deamon functions as an advisor to the pupils in difficult and stressfull situations.

Wearable: The goal of the wearable is to ensure that the pupils apply what they have learned in the game SelfCity to situations in everyday life. The wearable connects the pupils in real life to their deamon of the game. It also reminds them of the social skills they have acquired in the game, and intervene and support the user when it senses through biosensors when the pupils are in pressured social situations.
