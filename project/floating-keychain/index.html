Floating keychain
Thomasdw
Tue, 02/04/2014

I needed a floating keychain for my boat keys. I wanted a boat, so that when your keys are dropped in the water you'll see a boat sailing along. To my surprise this could not be found on the Internet, so I decided to make my own which is more fun anyway.

I used solidworks to create a stylised hollow 3D model of a ship. The interior is compartmentalized to increase strength and buoyancy when damaged. Inside there is a sort of tunnel where a rope can be threaded through to attach keys to.Â 

I want to use the 3D printer to make the ship. As I understood the ship can't be hollow but has to be filled with a sort of spiderweb pattern of plastic threads. I will choose the most "open" setting for this web as air content of the ship is directly related to buoyancy. De exact settings will be added later when I have started printing.
