Isis project
hypocritus
Mon, 10/17/2011

The original picture is a .jpg with 32,3 kb. I used Illustrator software. The materials are a  30 x 20 x 1.5 cm birch cutting board and a sheet of gold paper. The lasercutter was set on maximum power and the cutting proces of the wood took about half an hour (which smelled very nice). The inside of the outer lines from the picture was cut out of the paper which gave me the golden text inlay. These I fitted in with good old wood glue. Job done.
