Making stickers
Arcade
Fri, 03/27/2015

Been learning on the vinyl cutter. I wanted to reproduce my mascotte over this new machine I had never tried.

The vinyl cutter is a friendly one and gives you quite instant pleasure and quick results.

You might encounter technical issues with the cutting pressure, and different results with mat and glossy paper (I found mat paper more steady), but once you arranged you master-settings it's thrilling to see your work progress so fast. A clean source file is also important,and you might want to set it to maximize a certain flow in the movements of the blade.

Before used to hand draw and hand cut them, I wanted to speed up production times without loosing the expression of the original sign that's why I loved

**LOVED how the movement of my hand is replicated by the machine!

So I was able to produce some hundreds little creatures which will soon start polluting every (smooth) surface!
