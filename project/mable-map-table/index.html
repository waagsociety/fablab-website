Mable, the map table
michaelkaisers
Wed, 02/04/2015

This project describes the construction of a map-engraved table, that spans the width of a bed and is movable on caster wheels.

List of parts

  • 18mm thick wooden boards, ca. 30 x 70cm, and 30 x bed-width
  • 4 caster wheels
  • 4 Metal hooks
  • 6 Dowels
  • Screws
  • Oil

Tools needed

  • Drill
  • Screwdriver
  • Sandpaper
  • Cloth
  • Laser engraver
  • Â 

Assembly instructions

  1. Cut wooden boards into appropriate pieces, adjusting the sides height and top width to the specific bed, so that one can still sit compfortably below the table.
  2. Attach caster wheels to the bottom of each side on the extreme ends.
  3. Drill holes and connect the sides to the top with three dowels on each side, with the top board resting on the sides.
  4. Take apart and laser engrave the sides, e.g. using a digital image of a city map, to create Mable, the Map Table. Laser engraving settings: speed 80%, intensity 100%.
  5. Oil all surfaces and re-assemble.
  6. Enjoy breakfast in bed :)
