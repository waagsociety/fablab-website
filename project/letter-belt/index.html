Letter belt
stevensambell
Thu, 02/26/2015

This project is about laser-engraving a sequence of symbols which should be centered along the length of a belt. The challenges are to center these letters in a consistent way on this felxible material, while using the correct laser settings that should ensure a lasting engraving.

To get the engraving of each letter at the exact same position along the belt, I made a frame in which I fixed parts of the belt in a straight way. This way the belt was perfectly straight while the laser engraved the letters, 4 letters at a time. After each 4 letters, I moved the belt 10 centimeters further down the frame, ready for the next 4 letters.

The setting I used were: speed at 80% and power ate 60%. During a trial run I used a lower power percentage of 30 percent. But I found that the resulting engraving wasn't deep enough, I wasn't sure if it would be long-lasting enough.

I'm happy with the end-result, the letters are perfectly centered and very clear.
