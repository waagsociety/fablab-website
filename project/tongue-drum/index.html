Tongue Drum
product | documentation
alex
Tue, 04/22/2014

For the fab9 world contest, I decided to make a tongue drum from scratch. This is a nice compact drum with 12 tones. The design I found on the Internet, so I just had to draw and machine the box in the right measurments. This is crucial in order to get a proper sound. Since I made this during the fab9 conference in Yokohama, I really didnt had much time and ended up doing night shifts. But I managed just in time, I took the clamps off 2 hours before the performance took place. Here is a sound example, I was quite pleased how good it sounds, considering that I had no time to even tune it and practice, and I am not a drummer.
