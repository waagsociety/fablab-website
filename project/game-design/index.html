I have been developing a variety of game and toy prototypes at the waag over the past few years. My goal is to create cheap/creative toys and games for developing countries and the first world.

============================================

My new project is also game related but more in the sense of a real playable board game. My first idea was to create a web based game, but I realized very quickly that I didnt have the resources to do this so I reasoned that making a board game version might be a way to leverage my idea. The game is relatively simple. You control a gang and try to take over territory. I am sing the laser cutter to make prototype game pieces and board. As you can see in the image. The tiles are square with centre circles cut out. In the centre there will be information which is revealed like weapons and other attributes.

I am producing the prototype on cardboard (1.5mm thick) and the settings for the cutting are speed 4,power 100, ppi 400 and for engraving the settings are speed 70, power 100, ppi 750. I have found from extensive testing that these setting work best and fastest.

For those interested, you can watch the progress at gameseverywhere.nl (there is also contact information there).
